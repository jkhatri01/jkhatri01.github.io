MSc Thesis: Short term lung function impact from exposure to UFP

This thesis analysis short term impact on lung function from exposure to UFP (Ultrafine particles) in kerbside and urban background conditions simultaneously by undertaking bicycling as commute choice. UFP concentrations and breathing flow rates are measured continiously in realtime during bicycling. Pre/post bicycling activity impact on lung function in terms of spirometry and change in FeNO (Fractional exhaled Nitric Oxide)
